7 ChatGPT Extensions That Can Literally Make You Money in 2026

The 7 Extensions With Real Money Potential

Most ChatGPT extensions are toys. They make you marginally more productive. Great. But they don't make you money. These 7 are different. They're actual revenue streams. People have built £1K-50K/month businesses around each one. Here's the breakdown:

📧
1. Gmail Integration
The highest-revenue extension right now
Integrate ChatGPT directly into Gmail. Auto-summarize emails. Draft responses. Prioritize inbox. Every business executive gets 500+ emails/day. They hate email. They'll pay to fix it.
💰 Monthly Revenue Per Customer
£199-599
Tier 1: £199 (basic summaries) · Tier 2: £399 (+ auto-responses) · Tier 3: £599 (+ priority support)
📊 Realistic Monthly Income
£2K-8K/month
10-20 customers at average £350/month. Easy to scale with VA handling management.
⏱️ Time Per Customer/Month
30-60 mins
Setup (1 hr) + monthly monitoring/optimization. Can be partially automated.
🔍
2. Web Browsing + Perplexity Search
Research bottleneck = money opportunity
Give ChatGPT the ability to browse the web and search deep. Research companies, analyze competitors, track trends. Investment teams, business development managers, market researchers will pay serious money for this.
💰 Per-Project Revenue
£1.5K-4K
Custom market research report (5-6 hours work). Sell at £2K-5K each depending on complexity.
📊 Realistic Monthly Income
£3K-12K/month
2-5 projects/month at average £3K each. High-margin, project-based revenue.
⏱️ Time Per Project
5-7 hours
Research (2 hrs) + analysis (2 hrs) + report writing (1-3 hrs).
✍️

Which Extension Pays Best?

Highest revenue per hour: Web Browsing research reports (£2K-4K per 6 hours = £333-667/hr). Most scalable: Google Docs content (4 clients × £4K = £16K/month, manageable). Best recurring revenue: Gmail integration (stable £200-400/customer/month × 20 customers = £4K-8K/month). Least time: Slack integration (10 mins/customer/month after build, 99% passive).

💡 Strategy: Pick ONE extension. Get 3-5 paying customers. Prove it works. Then add second extension. Don't build all 7 at once. Master 1, scale to £2-3K/month, then expand. This is how people go from £0 to £10K+/month.
⚠️ Reality Check: First Customer Is Hardest

Getting customer #1 takes weeks of pitching. Getting customer #5 takes 2-3 conversations (word of mouth). By customer #10, people are inbound. The grind is month 1-2. After that, it compounds. Push through the hardest 2 months and revenue becomes predictable.
