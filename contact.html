Response Time

We aim to respond to all messages within 2–3 business days. For time-sensitive matters, Instagram DM is usually the fastest channel.

Editorial Inquiries

If you have a story idea, article pitch, or would like to collaborate on content, please include a brief description of the topic and your background when reaching out. We read everything, though we cannot guarantee a response to every submission.

What to Include

For the clearest response, mention the article or subject you are contacting us about, the outcome you have in mind, and any useful deadline. Collaboration proposals should include the intended audience, format, publishing channel, and relevant links or examples. Please do not send confidential information, attachments from unknown file-sharing services, or requests for paid placement without clearly identifying them.

Editorial Scope

Putera Gani focuses on thoughtful work about technology, design, culture, science, health, business, and the ideas shaping how people live and work. Corrections and factual feedback are especially welcome. When reporting an issue, include the page URL and the passage that needs review so we can investigate it accurately.
