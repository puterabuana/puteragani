Privacy Policy

How we handle information on this platform — clearly and transparently.

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Putera Gani ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website at puteragani.com (the "Site").

Information We Collect

Putera Gani is a static editorial website. We do not operate any user accounts, registration systems, or email collection systems. The Site does not store personal data on our own servers.

When you visit the Site, standard web server logs may record your IP address, browser type, referring pages, and pages visited. This information is used solely for technical purposes and is not linked to personally identifiable information.

Third-Party Services

The Site uses the following third-party services, which may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Fonts — Used for typography. Google may collect request data when fonts are loaded. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Unsplash — Article images are served from Unsplash CDN. See Unsplash Privacy Policy.
  • Tailwind CSS CDN — CSS framework loaded from CDN. Standard CDN request logs may apply.

Cookies

The Site itself does not set any cookies. Third-party services embedded in the Site (such as Google Fonts) may set their own cookies in accordance with their policies.

External Links

Articles and pages on this Site may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies when you visit them.

Social Media Sharing

The Site includes share buttons for Twitter/X and LinkedIn. Clicking these buttons will take you to those platforms, where their respective privacy policies apply. We do not receive personal data from these interactions.

Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Site constitutes acceptance of any changes.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us at ganiputerabuana@gmail.com.
